Subject view

Examiner view

In one window (or device) click on the "subject view".. It will open a big screen with an ID in the upper left hand corner. This is the "patient" view.

In another device, click "Examiner view." It should connect automatically and get its own ID. Then type in the 6-digit number of the first device (subject) into the box in the upper left hand-corner and click "Connect."
